Fried Rice Recipe

Chinese Fried Rice

A quick fried rice recipe like
you get at your favorite Chinese restaurant.
This fried rice is so good, just like grandma made it.

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup chopped baby carrots
  • ½ cup frozen green peas
  • Cooking oil
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 3 large eggs
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Assemble ingredients.
  2. Place carrots in a small saucepan and cover with water.
    Bring to a low boil and cook for 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in peas,
    then immediately drain in a colander.
  3. Heat a wok over high heat. Pour in vegetable oil,
    then stir in carrots, peas, and garlic; cook for about 30 seconds.
    Add eggs; stir quickly to scramble eggs with vegetables.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
  5. You can serve your fried rice with any beverage of your choice
